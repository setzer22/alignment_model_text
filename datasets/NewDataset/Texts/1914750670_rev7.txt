The account creation process at this bank proceeds as follows.
The first steps in the process are performed by the bank customer.
After visiting the bank website, she fills in her personal data and generates a PDF document with all the information relevant to this account request.
After printing this PDF, the customer needs to visit a post office to send these documents.
The post officer will check the identity of the customer and mail the account to the bank.
At the bank, the person in charge of making a decision on account requests is the bank officer.
Once he receives a new request, he'll check the customer data.
Depending on the available information and his own criteria, he may either accept or reject the request.
In the former case, he will mail the account documents to the customer.
In the latter, he will mail a letter of refusal.
